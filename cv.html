My History & Profile

I'm a 33 year old web designer and developer from Buenos Aires, Argentina. At the time I live in sunny Córdoba, where I work side-by-side with backend developers and designers in the product department of MercadoLibre. Going more specific, I'm in charge of the frontend inside the Shipping project but also I deliver feedback to others projects, such as MercadoLibre, MercadoPago and Classifieds departments.

I like what I do and I try to become better every day.
